Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • salt
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 6 tablespoons of salted butter

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into 1 to 2 inch pieces. Place potatoe pieces into a large pot.
  2. Fill pot with cold water. Dump the water out. Repeat 2-3 more times until water runs clear.
  3. Bring potatoes and water to a boil. Reduce to simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Strain potatoes and return to pot.
  5. Add butter and mash potatoes.
  6. Mound potatoes in the middle of the pot, add milk, raise heat to bring to a simmer.
  7. Remove potatoes from heat and mix. Salt to taste.
