Pasta Salad

  • 1 box of the Suddenly Pasta Salad cooked and drained(with packets)
  • 2 or more cups of Italian dressing(to your taste)
  • 8 oz of cubed cheddar cheese
  • 2 pounds of cherry or grape tomatoe halves
  • 7 oz of chopped ham or salami
  • Sprinkle in Italian seasoning
