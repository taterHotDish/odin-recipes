Butternut Squash

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter

Instructions

  1. Cut squash in half and remove seeds.
  2. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes with the cut side down.
  3. Turn over and put half of the sugar and half the butter in each squash. Bake an additional 30 minutes.
